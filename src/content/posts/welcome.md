## $ echo "hello, world"

Welcome to **sudo make vibe** — a blog about the intersection of creative coding and systems thinking.

### What to Expect

**Vibe Coding** — Using AI as a creative partner, tools like Cursor and Copilot

**Raspberry Pi** — Homelab setups, GPIO projects, self-hosting

### The Philosophy

This blog exists because I believe the best ideas emerge when you combine:

- The **creative flow** of vibe coding
- The **discipline** of systems engineering
- The **joy** of building with hardware
